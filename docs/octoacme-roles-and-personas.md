# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, coach the team on agile practices, and remove blockers that impede progress. They serve the team by fostering a collaborative environment and ensuring adherence to agile principles.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Coach team members on agile methodologies and self-organization
- Identify and remove impediments to team progress
- Shield the team from distractions and external interruptions
- Track team metrics (velocity, burndown) and promote continuous improvement
- Collaborate with Product Managers on backlog refinement

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and flow
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement
- Ensure transparency and collaboration across the team
- Reduce cycle time and improve predictability

### Typical Communication
- Daily facilitation of standups and team ceremonies
- Weekly metrics reviews and retrospective action tracking
- Ad-hoc blocker resolution and stakeholder coordination
- Coaching conversations with team members

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure user-centric design and usability throughout the project lifecycle. They research user needs, design intuitive interfaces, and validate solutions through testing and feedback.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Collaborate with Product Managers on feature requirements
- Work with Developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Establish and maintain design systems and accessibility standards
- Validate designs through user feedback and analytics

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and improve satisfaction metrics
- Ensure consistency across product interfaces
- Balance user needs with technical and business constraints

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings and persona updates
- Collaboration with Developers on implementation details
- Stakeholder presentations of design concepts

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts analyze project and product data to inform decision-making. They provide insights on performance, user behavior, and project health to guide strategy and improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Analyze user behavior and engagement metrics
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Identify trends, patterns, and anomalies in data
- Support A/B testing and experimentation
- Provide data-driven recommendations for product improvements

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision-making across the organization
- Improve visibility into product and project performance
- Identify opportunities for optimization and growth
- Ensure data quality and accessibility

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metrics reviews and trend analysis
- Ad-hoc data requests and analysis
- Dashboard updates and KPI reports
- Collaboration with Product Managers on success metrics

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers prepare clear, accurate documentation for processes, guides, and user-facing materials. They ensure that technical concepts are accessible to the intended audience.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain user documentation, guides, and API references
- Document internal processes and best practices
- Collaborate with Developers and Product Managers on content
- Ensure documentation accuracy and consistency
- Organize and structure information architecture
- Review and edit technical content from subject matter experts

### Goals
- Provide clear, accurate, and up-to-date documentation
- Reduce support burden through self-service resources
- Ensure documentation accessibility and discoverability
- Maintain consistent voice and style across all documentation

### Typical Communication
- Content review sessions with subject matter experts
- Documentation planning with Product and Engineering leads
- User feedback analysis and documentation gap identification
- Style guide and standards discussions

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers manage deployment schedules, coordinate releases, and oversee incident management procedures. They ensure smooth, reliable releases and quick recovery from issues.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and coordinate release schedules across teams
- Manage deployment pipelines and release automation
- Coordinate go/no-go decisions for releases
- Oversee incident response and post-mortem processes
- Maintain release notes and change logs
- Track deployment metrics and success rates
- Ensure compliance with release policies and regulatory requirements

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, predictable releases with minimal disruption
- Reduce deployment failures and rollback frequency
- Improve mean time to recovery (MTTR) for incidents
- Maintain high availability and system stability

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings and go/no-go reviews
- Incident response coordination and post-mortems
- Deployment status updates and release announcements
- Collaboration with Engineering on deployment automation
